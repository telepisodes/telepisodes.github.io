Welcome to the official telepisodes API documentation

Telepisodes API was conceptualized to provide a better, safer and faster way in contributing link contents in the website. It is accessible for users of the site who wishes to contribute content to the site.

Endpoint

https://telepisodes.org/api/submit

Method

GET

Parameters

  • key - [api key, request this key by sending an email to suggest@telepisodes.net with reason for using the API]
  • t - TVMaze ID (e.g. tvm1234) or TheMovieDB Id (e.g. tmd1234)
  • s - Season (integer)
  • e - Episode (integer)
  • u - URL / Video link to submit

Note

The site is protected by a 3rd party provider and rate limiting is in place. Do not ABUSE the API. Telepisodes implements a one strike policy where abusers are permanently banned from the site if identified as abusing the API or violating any guidelines of the site.

Is Telepisodes.org the Go-To Platform for Streaming Your Favorite TV Series?

Online movie and TV show streaming has become a crucial aspect of our entertainment consumption in today's digital age. It's simple to overlook the existence of other, lesser-known websites that provide free access to a wide range of content when major players in the market like Netflix, Hulu, and Amazon Prime dominate the market. One such website is Telepisodes.org, which makes the idea that viewers may watch their preferred TV shows for free online.

What is Telepisodes.org?

Telepisodes.org is an online platform designed to help users watch TV shows and movies without the need for a subscription or sign-up fee. The site thrives on user contributions, allowing visitors to submit content such as links to episodes, video streams, or entire seasons of popular TV shows and movies. By using various TV and movie APIs, the site aggregates content from multiple sources, offering users a seemingly vast library of entertainment. This crowdsourced approach makes it easy for the site to update its content regularly. Fans can submit new episodes, ensuring that the platform is constantly up-to-date with the latest releases. Whether you’re looking for the latest blockbuster movie or an obscure TV show from the 90s, you can likely find it on Telepisodes.org.

How It Works

Telepisodes.org operates on a peer-driven model, meaning much of its content is user-submitted. Users can upload links to video streams, allowing others to watch shows directly from the platform. Additionally, it connects with multiple TV and movie APIs to automate the process of adding new content. This integration enables it to continuously provide fresh and popular content in real-time. Unlike premium streaming services, Telepisodes.org doesn’t actually host content. Instead, it acts as an aggregator by providing links to external sources. When users search for a specific TV show or movie, the site pulls together available links from across the web.

Advantages of Using Telepisodes.org

  • Free Access: Unlike paid streaming platforms, Telepisodes.org offers a no-cost alternative for users wanting to watch their favorite shows and movies.
  • Crowdsourced Content: The ability for users to submit new episodes or movies keeps the site constantly updated.
  • Wide Selection: Thanks to the various APIs it taps into, Telepisodes.org provides an impressive selection of content across different genres and release dates.
